Frogs are in danger!!!

Altough frogs are really not something you could call a priority on the subject of endangered animals. They do suffer from the concequences of men. Like other animals, Frogs live in nature. Because of the destruction of their habitat due to the creation of cities and farmland frogs have less and less space to live.

Solutions...

Frogs need to be able to have their own living space. To maintain frogs is to maintain the entire ecosystem of Earth. Which is getting a hard blow due to pollution of the air, sea and land. So if we solve climat change and the problem of pollution. Not only frogs wil flourish but the entire earth will profit.
