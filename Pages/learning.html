The Basics

Frogs belong to the order Anura. Frog officially is a term that belongs to the Ranidae family but is broadly used to destinguish frogs and toads.
A frog is a member of the Anura order who has smooth-skin that leaps to move. A toad on the other hand is a warty skinned member of Anura that hop to move.

Some fun species of frogs

I covered the four frog species that I find most interesting

Red Eyed Tree Frog

This frog is very famous. It lives in rainforest where it hides in the top of trees to ambush flies, moths and crickets with its long sticky tongue.

Poison Dart Frog

Posion Dart Frog come in lots of colors. Yellow, red, orange, green and more! The poison dart frog is very poisonous. It has enough poison to kill 20.000 mice!

Reticulated Glass Frog

This frog is very cool because its skin is see-trough on the underside. You can even see its innir organs! This frog lives in the rainforest along streams.

Bullfrog

This frog is simply very big. It lives in swamps, ponds and lakes in northern america. It can get 20 centimeters big.
